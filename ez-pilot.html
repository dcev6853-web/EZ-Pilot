❓ FAQ

Questions, answered

How does the 1-month free trial work?
Sign up for the Pro annual plan and get your first month free — no credit card required. At the end of the trial, your annual plan starts at $19/month. The free trial is only available on annual billing; monthly plans have no trial.
Can I cancel anytime?
Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Annual plans are 12-month commitments — you won't be able to cancel mid-term, but you save ~17% and lock in the lower price for the whole year.
Which AI models do you use?
All 9 major models: Claude Opus 4.6, Claude Sonnet 4.6, Claude Sonnet 3.7, GPT-5, OpenAI o1, Gemini 2.5 Pro, Grok 4, Mistral Large, and Perplexity. Pick your favorite directly in chat.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+) and at rest. We use Firebase Auth + Firestore with strict per-user security rules. API keys for AI providers live on our backend and never touch your browser. We never sell personal data.
What can EZ Pilot actually do?
Reply to emails, schedule meetings, book flights, launch and optimize ad campaigns on 8 networks, draft and save Google Docs, review GitHub PRs, analyze budgets, control smart home devices, reply to DMs, and much more. Anything you can do across 50+ connected apps, EZ Pilot can draft and execute for you.
Does EZ Pilot act on its own?
By default, EZ Pilot suggests actions and you confirm with one tap. You can turn on full auto-execute for trusted workflows, and always see a complete AI Work Log of every action taken on your behalf.
What about file uploads?
Pro users get 15 file uploads per 24 hours. Max users get unlimited uploads. Any file type is supported — PDFs, spreadsheets, images, code, audio, video — and the AI can process them directly in chat.
